The Problem

⚡

🌎

Regional Inconsistency

Sports Department

Full Scouting Process

End-to-end workflow — from club methodology to final recommendation. 5 structured steps across 3 phases.

Phase 1 · What to Look For?
STEP 01

Club Methodology

Define game style: attacking phase, defensive phase, pressing, set pieces. Align with Technical Director on club identity.

STEP 02

Season Overview

End-of-season meeting: review collective + positional data. Output: positions needed and ideal attributes — no names yet.

Phase 2 · Analysis
STEP 03

Data Analysis

StatsBomb, Driblab, Impect, SkillCorner — per 90 stats, rankings by position, league comparison, age curve projection.

STEP 04

Reference Category

AI similarity tools: who plays like Player X? Cross-league comparisons using Impect + StatsBomb models.

Phase 3 · Decision
STEP 05

Candidates Comparison

Side-by-side: league benchmarks, global benchmarks, scouted targets vs. current roster positional needs.

Performance

Sport Science Department

Bridges data, physiology, and football performance. Supports coaching staff with objective metrics, player load management, and evidence-based protocols.

📡

Load Monitoring

GPS tracking, ATL/CTL ratios, HRV trends. Daily risk flags per player. Prevents overtraining and injury spikes.

🩺

Injury Prevention

Pattern identification from physical testing. Thermography CPK baseline. Return-to-play protocols with clear criteria.

🔄

Recovery Protocols

Structured recovery sessions between fixtures. Sleep, cold water immersion, and nutrition timed to match schedule.

🏋️

Physical Testing

Pre-season: strength, endurance, jump, speed, asymmetry. Anthropometric baseline (fat mass, muscle, hydration).

🥗

Nutrition & Hydration

Individual diet plans per player. Match-day nutrition protocol. Hydration tracking integrated with GPS load data.

😴

Sleep & Wellbeing

Sleep quality monitoring. Wellness questionnaires pre-training. Psychological readiness integrated with load decisions.

Staff Structure

Head of Sport Science

Leads the department. Designs annual monitoring plans, oversees testing batteries, communicates directly with Head Coach and SD.

Performance Analyst

Processes GPS and physical data. Builds load dashboards and flags injury-risk players daily using ATL/CTL and HRV trends.

Physiotherapist

Manages rehabilitation protocols. Works with performance staff to clear players from injury and design return-to-play criteria.

Nutritionist

Individual plans per player. Match-day nutrition protocols. Works with the hydration specialist on game-week structures.

S&C Coach

Strength and conditioning programs. Manages gym sessions, in-season maintenance loads, and pre-season physical block.

Psychologist

Mindset, pressure management, team cohesion, and individual player support. Integrated in the weekly staff meeting.

Financial Planning

Budgeting Framework

An end-to-end, automated budgeting platform — from framework definition through execution and in-year adjustments — ensuring consistency, transparency, and governance across all stakeholders.

Why This Matters

Current finance processes in football clubs are highly manual, fragmented, and heavily dependent on individuals — leading to inefficiencies, errors, and lack of traceability. Budgeting cycles can extend over months, consuming significant time from both finance and business teams. This platform eliminates that friction.

🏗️

Budget Framework Configuration

Finance teams centrally define and govern the full budget structure and parameters.

  • ›Define budget methodologies, cycles, and timelines
  • ›Distribute standardized guidelines and templates
  • ›Maintain single source of truth for all planning assumptions
Outcome: Controlled, standardized foundation for budget creation
📥

Guided Budget Data Collection

Structured, user-friendly interfaces for cost center budget preparation.

  • ›Distribute dynamic working templates
  • ›Enable cost center owners to input budgets with embedded validation rules
  • ›Capture detailed justifications at the line-item level
  • ›Support versioning and iterative updates without loss of auditability
Outcome: Accurate, complete, fully justified budget submissions
✅

Embedded Review & Approval Workflows

The platform digitizes and enforces multi-level validation processes.

  • ›Collaborative reviews between finance and cost center owners
  • ›Automated checks for data consistency and assumption alignment
  • ›Route budgets through configurable approval hierarchies
  • ›Track feedback, revisions, and approvals in real time
Outcome: Streamlined validation with full transparency and governance
📊

Automated Consolidation & Executive Reporting

Aggregates all inputs into a unified financial view in real time.

  • ›Automatically consolidate budgets across cost centers and departments
  • ›Generate executive dashboards and board-ready reports
  • ›Maintain traceability from consolidated figures to individual inputs
Outcome: Faster, more accurate consolidation with enhanced decision support
🔗

Seamless ERP Integration & Budget Deployment

Ensures smooth transition from planning to execution. Integration via ERP Import/Export template (CSV format).

  • ›Validate and transform approved budgets for ERP compatibility
  • ›Automate budget upload into ERP systems
  • ›Notify stakeholders upon budget activation
  • ›Enable post-upload validation by cost centers
Outcome: Reduced manual effort with full alignment between planning and execution
🔀

Controlled Budget Adjustment Management

Governed, auditable in-year budget modifications.

BUDGET TRANSFERS
  • ›Initiate via standardized digital forms
  • ›Validate availability and enforce business rules
  • ›Route for multi-level approval, then auto-update ERP
BUDGET SUPPLEMENTS
  • ›Capture additional funding requests with full justification
  • ›Assess impact against overall budget availability
  • ›Apply changes directly in ERP upon approval
🛡️

End-to-End Governance, Visibility & Auditability

Across all stages, the platform provides:

  • ›Full audit trails of all changes, approvals, and decisions
  • ›Real-time visibility into budget status and variances
  • ›Role-based access control for secure collaboration
  • ›Data consistency across planning, approval, and execution
Outcome: Transparent, compliant, and fully governed budgeting environment
